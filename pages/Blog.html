Positive Impact of Technology in the Watch Industry

Technology in the field of watches has been of tremendous benefit in that it improves the degree of precision, sturdiness and usefulness of the watches. Technologies such as ceramics and carbon fiber have made watches last longer, due to their ability in avoiding easy wear out. Features like health monitors or GPS that make watches capable of doing much more than just telling time have broadened the application of watches. In addition, computer-based manufacturing and enhanced mobility enablement have raised precision and dependability making the watch industry an indicator of sophistication as well as technological advancement.

Negative Impact of Technology in the Watch Industry

The danger of technology has come into the watch industry along with a number of facilities that are offered by technology. Smartwatches are as popular with customers as mechanical or even luxury watches; they became that kind of trend allowing users to combine several devices into a single one. Such a change has affected the popular consumption of traditional watches, especially among current youth. Also, the use of technology has brought alarm for the loss of traditional craft and skills because of mechanized production. Finally, owing to technological development, smartwatches often experience planned obsolescence.
